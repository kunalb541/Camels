**Status: STRUCTURAL_BLOCKER**

The SubLink tree contains SnapNum 0–33 but stores only total halo mass (Mass, MassHistory) — not stellar mass, sSFR, or gas mass. Downloading the missing groups catalog is out of scope for this test run.

The forward/reverse gap cannot be computed from current data. This is reported as a structural blocker per the pre-registration protocol.
